# Thompson Road Station

![Thompson Road Station](../assets/gallery/thompson_road/wip.png){ width="750" }

Thompson Road Station (Chinese: 坦森路站) is a station on Line 1 in the Shanlu area. It is currently a work in progress as of January 11, 2026. It was opened around the start of September 2025 but was closed shortly after due to traffic issue in the trains.
