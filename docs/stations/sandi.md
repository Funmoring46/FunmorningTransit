# Sandi Station

![Exit A of Airport Terminal 1 Station](../assets/gallery/airport_terminal_one/stationentrance.png){ width="750" }

Sandi Station (Chinese: 三地站) is a station on the Mountains-Sandi Railway in the Shanlu area. It was opened around October 2025.

## History

Named after the 3 areas near the station, it still does not have an exit yet.
