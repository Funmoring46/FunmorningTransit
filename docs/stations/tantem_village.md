# Tantem Village Station

![Tantem Village Station](../assets/gallery/tantem_village/tantem_village.png){ width="750" }

Tantem Village Station (Chinese: 坛腾村站) is a station on the Line 1 and Tantem Test located in the Kuangzhen area. It was opened around October 2025.

## History

Named after the village nearby, it is the third biggest station in Kuangzhen and will have a transfer in the Kishimodo-Tantem Railway soon.
