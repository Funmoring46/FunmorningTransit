# Forest Station

* Not to be confused with Lakeside Forest station *

No image yet

Forsst Station (Chinese: 森林站) is a station on the Fast Line and the Village Express in the Shanlu area. It was the second tranfer station to open in the server. (first is mountains)

## History

It is named after the bamboo forest at on the exit of the station and it is also one of the stations to open during late August.
