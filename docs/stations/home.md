# Home Station

![Entrace of Home Station](https://github.com/Funmoring46/FunmorningTransit/blob/main/docs/assets/gallery/home/home.png?raw=true){ width="750" }

Home Station (Chinese: 家站) is a station on Home APM located on Shanlu area。 It is close to the cherry wood home nearby and opened around the end of August 2025.

## History

The first station to be created into the funmorning transit system, it is a very small station and was created just for fun but remains for historical purposes.
