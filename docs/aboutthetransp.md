# About the transportation

In the server you can use trains to go to different places but they are not all from the same company. Each company takes care of each part of the transit area.

Small summary of each of them:

**funmorning metro**: takes cares of line 1 and line 2, airport apm, boat apm, and line 3.

**lanna railway**: takes care of the mountains kiyama railway, mountains kuangzhen dong railway and the kuangzhen sandi railway.

**kuanggzhen railway**: takes care of the mountains kiyama railway, mountains kuangzhen dong railway and the kuangzhen sandi railway.

**Hanano railway (Sendai area)**: takes care of the mountains kiyama railway extension, mountains kiyama second railway extension, and the shoyondo-tantem railwway

---

There are regions in this world for different areas. This can affect borders and other stuff.

**Shanlu area**: the current area you might be in (mountains, spawn, airport, etc.)

**Kuangzhen area**: name of the village nearby the train station (kuangzhen, passport pickup, boston, etc.)

**Sendai area**: japan but my world (kiyama, kishimid≈ç, etc.)
