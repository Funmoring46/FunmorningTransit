# About the transportation

In the server you can use many modes of transport to go to different places but they are not all from the same company. Each company takes care of each part of the transit area.

Small summary of each of them:

**funmorning metro**: takes cares of line 1 and line 2, airport apm, home apm, fast line, village express, and line 3.

**lanna railway**: takes care of the mountains kiyama railway, mountains kuangzhen dong railway and the kuangzhen sandi railway.

**kuangzhen railway**: takes care of the kuangzhen boston railway, line 1 (kuangzhen) and the kuangzhen-kuangzhen east connector line (they are a subsidiary company of lanna railway)

**kuangzhen boats**: takes care of the kuanggzhen loop and tantem test.

**Hanano railway (Sendai area)**: takes care of the mountains kiyama railway extension, mountains kiyama second railway extension, and the shoyondo-tantem railway

---

There are regions in this world for different areas. This can affect borders and other stuff.

**Shanlu area**: the current area you might be in (mountains, spawn, airport, etc.)

**Kuangzhen area**: name of the village nearby the train station (kuangzhen, passport pickup, boston, etc.)
